Bio

My interests center around technology, multimedia art, education, and progressive politics. Since I moved to the Bay Area in 2009, I have split my time between web development and teaching in the Oakland Public School District. I volunteer at Media Enterprise Alliance in Oakland where I teach multimedia-arts, music, and technology to recent immigrants and underserved youth.
